Mental Health Check-Up

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by stress?




2. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?




3. How often do you find it difficult to concentrate or make decisions?




4. Do you feel socially isolated or disconnected from others?




5. In the last month, how often have you experienced changes in your sleep patterns (e.g., difficulty falling asleep, waking up early)?




6. How often do you experience feelings of anxiety or nervousness?




7. How often do you engage in activities that you enjoy?




8. How would you rate your overall mood in the last few weeks?




9. How often do you have negative thoughts about yourself or your life?




10. Have you been able to cope with your daily responsibilities (work, family, personal tasks)?
